## Summary of Available Reviews with Photos

### Sources with Client Photos:
1. **Google Maps** - 24 reviews, most have Google profile photos
2. **CrushMyTicket.com** - 4 visible reviews with profile photos
3. **Avvo** - 17 reviews but uses anonymous avatars (no real photos)

### Key Findings:
- Google Maps reviews have the best profile photos (real Google accounts)
- CrushMyTicket has some profile photos but lower quality
- Avvo uses generic avatars, not real client photos

### Recommended Approach:
Since Google and CrushMyTicket reviews have profile photos but these are personal user photos that cannot be downloaded/used without permission, the best approach is to:
1. Use the review TEXT content (which is public)
2. Use initials or generic professional avatars for the testimonials
3. Cite the source (Google, Avvo, CrushMyTicket) for credibility
